    Your Success Is My Passion

    I am on A Mission to help you develop a Powerful Mind & discover a Passion-Life

    About me

    • I like to think of myself as a writer, a story-teller. Since I was a kid, seeing the thoughts in my mind take structure into words, sentences, opinions… that has always been my zone! But embracing this identity of mine was not a very straightforward journey.

    Who am I?

    • On the professional front, I am a passion-mind coach. You would think that as a coach, I know all about how the mind processes, passion and how one can channel their passion to enlighten their minds. However, me? I am still exploring my mind. I am still in the process of establishing a deeper connect with my mind, so that I can recognize my passion areas better and utilize their true potential. And I will help you do exactly that, for yourself.

    What do I do?

    • I help people find their passion. I help people expand their mind. I help people cultivate an expansive mindset that will enable them to take control of their lives. Surprisingly, everything one needs to recognize their true passion is already within them. But a cluttered mind, often overwhelmed by the ways of a demanding world, finds it difficult to recognize what’s worthy of attention, and what’s not. It is a pity that most of us are not able to recognize our passion all throughout our lifetime. This is where I come in!

    How did I find my passion?

    • How did I find my passion? Let me take you on a quick journey through my life. As an introvert with low self-esteem, I wasn’t one of the cool kids in school. In spite of being an intelligent kid, I wasn’t the most expressive one. Hailing from a middle-class family, I was bullied for not being able to afford luxuries that my friends could. As you can imagine, it wasn’t the most conducive environment for a kid’s intellectual growth. However, since early childhood, I felt power whenever I was writing. With a pen in my hand, and a paper to pour my thoughts into, I could see purpose! Add to that a grandpa with whom story-time was a daily fixture! It developed in me a deep love for story-telling and writing. I enjoyed an innate sense of satisfaction when I could give structure to the myriad thoughts in my mind, in the form of words on paper! You must be wondering if I paid heed to what my mind was trying to tell me and followed the voice of my passion! I did not. I became a sales professional. In the early days of my career, I excelled in writing emails, reports and preparing presentations. But I was fired. Twice. Why? Because I could not ‘sell’. Neither a product, nor myself. How could I? Sales wasn’t my passion! So, for the next many years, I slogged. I gained considerable success, my paychecks got fatter as I got promotions and reached a stage in my career where theoretically life should have looked good. But it did not. There was nothing to stimulate my mind. The mundane regularity of my professional life started crippling my psyche. It was as if my mind had announced a lockdown on itself. That had a disastrous effect on my personal life, and I ended up with a divorce. I had hit rock-bottom. There was no way out of that pit but to climb back up. In a desperate attempt to gain back my life, I attended a seminar one day, which talked about how a simple mindset change can change the complete picture of one’s life. It intrigued me. I started reading up on mindset, met experts and started my journey towards decoding my mind. In no time, I could feel the ripple effect on my life. I realised, my existence till then had been devoid of any meaning! Finally, my mind was asking me the right questions. “Why are you here on Earth ? What is your purpose here?” I realised, I had been chasing down the wrong path. My mind always knew what it wanted. To tell stories. Stories that could inspire, stories that could enlighten, stories that could invigorate people. So I started rewriting my story. And here I am today, helping you do the same.

    What have I learnt?

    • The mind knows what it wants. A voice inside your head will keep whispering to you about your passion. All you need to do is cut out the noise and listen. Running down the wrong path, you will only end up a misfit. And the more you try to fit in, the heavier the burden will be on you. The only way out is to rediscover your real self. That is when you will find your purpose in life.
